    Fountain University Academoral Centre

    We are in the service of responding to the menace of drug abuse and addiction problems with a view to protecting the interest of impressionable youthful students and others as well as redeeming those who have consciously or unconsciously fallen victims.

    Support Us Contact Us

    Our Mission

    To give a comprehensive regime of University education driven by innovation and entrepreneurship to the youthful generation with behavioural and substance abuse challenges.

    Our Vision

    To be one of the leading Centre providing coordinated string of services, which include training, rehabilitation, skill acquisition, and entrepreneurial empowerment on a sustainable basis for the latter category of students and youth.

    Our Key Objective

    To offer impressionable youthful students opportunities for self-reflection, deployment of potentials for positive engagement, innovation, ethical rebirth, and entrepreneurial development.
